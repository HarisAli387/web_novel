About the Author

Welcome to My Novels — a digital home of imagination and stories that touch hearts. Here, every novel is written with passion, emotion, and a deep connection to everyday life. My aim is to create stories that you can not only read, but feel.

I'm Haris, an aspiring writer and storyteller. I started writing from a young age, inspired by the people, places, and dreams that surround us. From mysterious tales to emotional journeys — each story you read here carries a piece of my heart.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Keep reading, keep imagining, and never stop dreaming.
